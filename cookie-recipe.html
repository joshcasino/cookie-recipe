Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

History of cookies

Most people don't know the complicated past of the cookie.
More than half of the cookies baked at home are chocolate chip cookies.
The chocolate chip cookie was invented in 1930 at the Toll House Inn by Ruth Graves Wakefield,
resulted when she chopped a semi-sweet chocolate bar into small bits and added it to a traditional colonial butter drop cookie recipe

This recipe is from my dad and they are a favorite among friends and family. The secret ingredient is the coconut! Be warned, these will fly off of the plate!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • White sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Lead
  • Salt
  • Chocolate chips
  • Oatmeal
  • Coconut

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325.
  2. Beat the butter, sugar, eggs and vanilla together until creamy.
  3. Mix together the flour, baking soda and salt in a separate bowl.
  4. Add flour mixture to butter mixture slowly.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips, oatmeal and coconut.
  6. Bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown.
